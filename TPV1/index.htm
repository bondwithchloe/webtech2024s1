About Me

My mission is to promote a people-first mentality by sparking game-changing human connections in our virtual world.

With the massive shift to remote/hybrid workplaces, people have become *disconnected* from their co-workers & company culture. At BOND, we *reconnect* remote teams & rebuild unique online company cultures by sparking virtual water-cooler chats through a daily gamified BONDing question.

I am pursuing a B.S. in Business Administration and Management with a concentration in Technology & Entrepreneurship at Babson College. As a trilingual/multinational citizen, I strive to empower individuals & companies on a global scale. I thrive in high-performing environments characterized by intense competition and actively seek connections with fellow risk-takers who share my passions in entrepreneurship, gamification, AI, and the future of work.

BONDing Fact: My favorite movie quote, "Just Keep Swimming" from Finding Nemo, serves as a personal mantra, reflecting my unwavering determination and resilience in facing adversity.
