About Me

Hi, I'm Chloe Samaha.
Thanks for dropping by :) You are about to learn A LOT about me

Most importantly, I want you to know this: My current obsession is understanding what it takes to unlock someone's full potential.

I uncovered this after I landed my dream job at a women-led PE firm in NYC.
I was incredibly frustrated with the limitations of current workplace dynamics, which stifled my potential and ability to make an impact.

So I started building the solution for it: BOND, the Salesforce for managers.

Now that you have a trailer, enjoy the movie!

I always love connecting with like-minded people. So if you believe that you have a piece to the puzzle, reach out!
